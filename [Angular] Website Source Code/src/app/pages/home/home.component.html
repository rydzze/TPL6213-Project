STRACE

Making System Calls Cool Again
Ever wondered about the name "Strace"? It's an abbreviation for "system call trace," functioning like a digital detective that tracks a program's journey through the operating system.

Why "Strace"? It signals our interest in understanding how machines communicate, providing insight into the realm of tech processes. So, when you encounter "Strace," think of our commitment to unravel the language of software, exploring the intricacies of technology one step at a time.
